What Is the Dae Encyclopedia of Patterns?

The Dae Encyclopedia of Patterns is a structured, evidence-informed reference library of common psychological, behavioral, cognitive, emotional, and relational patterns. It is designed to help users quickly compare patterns by definition, observable behaviors, short-term psychological function, long-term costs, likely origins, evidence strength, and evidence-based intervention options.
